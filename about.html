About Us

Who We Are & What We Stand For

H.O.P.E. was founded by Norna Augustave and Gifty Myers to provide comprehensive contracting and consulting services. With a focus on efficiency, quality, and compliance, we serve government and private sector clients, delivering solutions that create measurable impact.

Our Leadership

Norna Augustave

Managing Director
Norna is the Managing Director of House of Paradise Enterprises, where she leads the organization with a hands-on, service-driven approach to government contracting. She is directly involved in strategic planning, contract oversight, and client engagement, ensuring every project aligns with agency objectives and regulatory requirements.

With a strong understanding of public-sector standards, Norna prioritizes compliance, thorough documentation, risk management, and accountability across all contracts. She ensures that each engagement is carefully planned, properly resourced, and executed with precision. Her leadership emphasizes operational efficiency, fiscal responsibility, and full transparency, recognizing the importance of safeguarding taxpayer resources.

Norna is committed to building strong, collaborative relationships with government agencies and partners. She believes successful contracting is rooted in trust, consistency, and performance, not just task completion. Under her direction, House of Paradise Enterprises delivers reliable services while maintaining the highest ethical and professional standards.

Gifty Myers

Co-Managing Director
Gifty Myers is the Co-Managing Director of House of Paradise Enterprises (H.O.P.E.), bringing a strong commitment to service, strategic thinking, and results-driven leadership. She is an accomplished entrepreneur and consultant known for helping organizations and individuals navigate complexity, identify opportunities, and achieve sustainable growth.

With a client-centered approach, Gifty delivers tailored consulting solutions that strengthen businesses and support professional and personal development. Her work is grounded in thoughtful strategy, practical insight, and trusted guidance, ensuring that each engagement creates measurable value.

Gifty believes that lasting success is built on integrity, meaningful relationships, and a clear sense of purpose. Service is central to her leadership philosophy, and her passion for empowering others continues to shape the vision, culture, and impact of the company.
